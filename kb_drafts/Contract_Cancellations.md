# Handling Contract Cancellations

If a customer requests to cancel or terminate their contract with Hadrius, Support must acknowledge the request and immediately route it to the appropriate internal team. **Support does not process contract cancellations directly.**

## Standard Operating Procedure

1. **Acknowledge the Request:**
   Reply to the customer confirming that their request has been received. You can use a template similar to this:
   > "Hi [Name], I have received your request to terminate your contract with Hadrius. I am looping in our Account Management/Billing team to process this and provide you with the next steps."

2. **Route the Ticket:**
   Add an internal note to the ticket and re-assign or escalate the ticket to the **Account Management** or **Billing** team (depending on your firm's specific assignment rules).

3. **Leave the Ticket Open:**
   Do not close the ticket until the Account Management team has confirmed the cancellation is fully processed.
