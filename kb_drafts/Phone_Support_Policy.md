# Phone Support Policy

Hadrius currently provides support primarily through asynchronous channels (email, Pylon tickets) and scheduled Zoom meetings. 

**If a customer requests a phone number to call Support:**
1. Politely inform them that we do not offer an inbound phone support line at this time.
2. Offer to help them async via the ticket thread.
3. If the issue is complex and requires live troubleshooting, offer to send them a calendar link (e.g., Calendly/HubSpot scheduling link) to book a live Zoom support session with a specialist.

*Draft Reply Example:*
> "Hi [Name], we don't currently have an inbound phone support line, but I'm more than happy to help you with this right here! If it would be easier to walk through this live, you can book a quick Zoom call with our team using this link: [Insert Scheduling Link]."
