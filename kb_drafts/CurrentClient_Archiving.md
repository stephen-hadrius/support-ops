# Connecting CurrentClient for Archiving

CurrentClient is a fully supported communication channel in Hadrius for SMS/text message archiving.

## Provisioning and Setup

To connect CurrentClient to Hadrius, the customer needs a specific **Journal Address** from us where they can feed their SMS data.

**When a customer requests to connect CurrentClient:**
1. You must request a unique CurrentClient Journal Address from the Hadrius technical/engineering team. 
2. Once the engineering team provisions the address, reply to the customer providing the journal address.
3. The customer will input this journal address into their CurrentClient admin settings to begin routing data to Hadrius.

*Note: Since the journal address is unique to each firm, Support cannot generate it autonomously and must escalate the ticket to engineering to get the address provisioned before resolving the ticket.*
